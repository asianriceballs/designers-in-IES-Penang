Product Management

Physical and Down-loadable Products

You can sell all sorts of products on your store. They could be physical products (like the BB-8 everyone wanted for Christmas but got a pair of socks instead) or down-loadable products like eBooks or MP3. Whichever type you choose to sell, we've made it easy for you to upload them to your store.

Bulk Upload Products

If you have a 100 or 1000 products to upload, just use our bulk upload feature and your products will populate your store in minutes, complete with images, pricing and description.

Manage Products

Our manage products page is packed with really cool features what will save you time and effort. For example, if you want to add a new product with a similar description to an existing product, you can just duplicate it in a couple of clicks and tweak the details later. Shifting products and making products appear in more than one category is accomplished with equal ease.
