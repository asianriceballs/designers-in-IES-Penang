Attract Visitors

Make it easy for customers to find you with fully optimized pages and links to your store from everywhere: marketplaces, Facebook, Twitter, and much more.

Optimized Pages and URLs

Your home page, category and product pages are automatically optimized for search engines. Schema integration means that your product and category pages will have ranking boosts on Google, and will show more relevant information to potential customers. Your InstanteStore will automatically update its Google Sitemap whenever you add new products or pages so that Google will know about your new products right away.

Social Signals Matter: Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, and Social Bookmarking

InstanteStore makes it easy for you to leverage the power of social media so your customers can market your products for you with the help of social buttons on your product pages.

Our Rich Pins integration makes your products even more attractive on Pinterest. Our Facebook Social Store app gives you a whole new avenue to market your products over Facebook.

Multiple Channels: Integration with Google Shopping and Lots of Other Marketplaces

InstanteStore is integrated with Google Shopping and other marketplaces such as Shopzilla, Shopping.com and more, so getting listed on these sites is easy. Each of these popular channels can provide potential customers with new ways to find your store.

Customization and Further Optimization Made Simple with Our SEO Wizard

The InstanteStore SEO Wizard makes it easy for you to further optimize your pages. It automatically measures and tracks your keyword usage and guides you as you add or modify your product content.
