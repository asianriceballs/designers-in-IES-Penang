Split Tests

The most successful sales funnels are a product of continuous testing and optimization. Our template split test feature lets you try out different variations of your store templates (a different color, perhaps, or a different banner on the left side, etc.) so that you can compare how each variation performs. You can then keep testing the "winning" split tests and keep improving your store's conversion rates.

Discount Coupons

If price is a factor, then discount coupons could give your potential customer just enough of an incentive to get them buying.

Sales

The right sale at the right time could entice a customer to make a purchase.

Aborted Order Handling

If a customer adds one of your products to their shopping cart and goes through checkout, but stops short of buying, there's likely a good reason for it, and more importantly, a compelling deal that you could offer to get them to change their minds. Our Aborted Order Handling feature lets you easily get in touch with almost-customers like these to make them offers they can't refuse.

Retaily Integration!

Beyond all of the many features that InstanteStore already provides, if you're looking for an extra edge, here it is: Retaily integration. Retaily provides all the tools you need to optimize your store's conversion and retention. Retaily uses product recommendations, urgency and scarcity messages, remarketing via ads and email, and much more to boost your store's conversion both before and even after a potential customer has left your store.

Increase Customer Value

It's simple: the more each customer buys, the more value you'll get out of your traffic spending.

Cross-sell

Some products lend themselves naturally to being cross-sold: if you buy a pair of shoes, you're likely to also be interested in some comfortable matching socks. Offering related products that make sense to a customer increases the chances of them making a larger order.

Cross-sell on Final Payment

Whether it's for customers with preferential pricing or bulk buyers, our wholesale features have you covered. You can easily set our wholesale module up to offer volume discounts or tiered discounts, giving customers an easy incentive to buy multiple units of your products.

Customer Repeat Orders

Customer loyalty goes beyond simply getting more value out of each customer. Repeat customers are more likely to return to your store and also to refer you to their friends, giving you a great source of new potential customers.

Points system

Reward customers for their purchases by awarding them points based on the value of products that they have bought. You can award the same number of points for all products or even award more (or less) points for specific products that you want to promote. This encourages customers to spend more so that they can redeem products with their points.

Newsletters/Discount Coupons

Constantly reach out to your customers to keep them up-to-date on your store’s latest offerings with our Newsletter Management and Broadcast tools. Combining our discount coupons with your newsletters is a potentially interesting way to stimulate repeat business.
