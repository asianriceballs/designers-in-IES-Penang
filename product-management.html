Product Management

Physical and Down-loadable Products

You can sell all sorts of products on your store. They could be physical products (like the BB-8 everyone wanted for Christmas but got a pair of socks instead) or down-loadable products like eBooks or MP3. Whichever type you choose to sell, we've made it easy for you to upload them to your store. For added convenience, you can use the InstanteStore App to check on your store and even upload products.

Place Products On Sale

The On Sale page is one of the most popular pages on any online store because everyone want to get a good bargain. We made it super easy for you to place particular products or even the whole store on sale if your wish.

Front Page Products

The front page is usually the page most visitors land on first when they visit a site. Featuring products on the front page can improve sales of those products, especially if it's a new product that no one knows about just yet. Recognizing this, we also made it convenient for you to place products on your front page. You can have the system display product which has been specifically assigned to front page or automatically display products from different product groupings â€“ categories, new products, top selling and on sale.

Bulk Upload Products

If you have a 100 or 1000 products to upload, just use our bulk upload feature and your products will populate your store in minutes, complete with images, pricing and description.

Manage Products

Our manage products page is packed with really cool features what will save you time and effort. For example, if you want to add a new product with a similar description to an existing product, you can just duplicate it in a couple of clicks and tweak the details later. Shifting products and making products appear in more than one category is accomplished with equal ease. Shifting categories is another cool feature that comes built-it to every InstanteStore. So if you want to turn a sub category into a main category, you can do it in a matter of seconds.
